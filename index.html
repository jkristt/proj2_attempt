Income Inequality in Chicago

__________________________________________

Mapping per capita income in Chicago's 77 community areas

The gap between the rich and the poor has never been as wide as it is today. In Chicago, these disparities are very noticeable geographically with high-income residents concentrated in the loop and many community areas north of the loop. Low-income residents, on the other hand, are mostly concentrated in the south and west sides and parts of the far north side of the City.
